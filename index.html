About Me

Hello World, My name is Toyosi Olabisi. I have two kids, both boys. My oldest is eleven and my youngest is four. They are the cutest and most annoying kids EVER!! I love playing soccer, pottery and traveling . I actually played in Denmark and Norway when I was fifteen then my knees happened. So, now I just live vicariously through Mallory Pugh and company. I am the seventh of nine children which is a bit strange because I'm also the oldest. I was born in Nigeria and moved to Saudi Arabia when I was eight. I moved to Austin, Tx., where I attended boarding school when I was fifteen years old.
